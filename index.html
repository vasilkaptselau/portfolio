Symphojazz orchestra project

My first project which I created when I took HTML/CSS class. I tried to apply my skills and built a website for symphonic orchestra with pictures of all musicians, event calendar, and music pieces (music files). Also, every player introduced himself and explained why he/she playes in the orchestra. Website contains a piece of orchestra history and contact form for all willing to join.
